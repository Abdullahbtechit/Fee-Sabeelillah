Privacy Policy for FeeSabeelillah

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Introduction

FeeSabeelillah ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • Phone Number: If you choose to provide your phone number during registration.
  • Quiz Data: Your quiz answers, scores, and progress.
  • Hifz Progress: Your Quran memorization progress and assignments.
  • Support Messages: Messages you send through our in-app support feature.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Track your quiz progress and scores
  • Track your Hifz (Quran memorization) progress
  • Enable instructors to monitor student progress
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Send notifications about quiz updates and assignments
  • Improve our services

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which employs industry-standard encryption and security measures.
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Data Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Instructors/Admins: If you are a student, your quiz scores and Hifz progress may be visible to assigned instructors and administrators.
  • Service Providers: We use Google Firebase for data storage and authentication.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for educational and religious purposes. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of notifications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase: For data storage and notifications (Firebase Privacy)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Delete Your Account & Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Upon receiving your request, we will:

  • Delete your account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Delete your quiz scores and history
  • Delete your Hifz progress and assignments
  • Delete your support messages
  • Remove all personal data from our systems

Processing Time: Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days.
